Capitol Reef National Park

Learn About the Park

Capitol Reef is a national park is located in south-central Utah, in the heart of red rock county.

3 Fun Facts About Capitol Reef National Park:

  • Capitol Reef is designated an International Dark Sky Park, offering some of the darkest night skies in the country, perfect for stargazing.
  • Capitol Reef National park has large fruit orchards, which visitors are allowed to pick fruit from when in season.
  • What makes the landscape of Capitol Reef so unique is the rocky unique formation that is “wrinkly” making it look like reefs that are found underwater. Hence the name.

What is there to do at Capitol Reef?

There is a lot to do and see at Capitol Reef, you can hike, camp, as well as go for a sceneic drive through Capitol Reef.
