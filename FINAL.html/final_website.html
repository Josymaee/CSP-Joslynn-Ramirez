Capitol Reef National Park

The adventure of a lifetime awaits you!

Capitol Reef National Park is the perfect destination for those who love exploring breathtaking landscapes! From colorful canyons and towering cliffs to rock formations that seem otherworldly, this park is a must-see.

Discover the Waterpocket Fold, a massive, 100-mile-long wrinkle in the Earth’s crust. Learn more about the Capitol Dome.

Discover More About the Park

Located in the heart of red rock country, Capitol Reef National Park is one of Utah’s best-kept secrets!

3 Fun Facts:

  • It's an International Dark Sky Park, perfect for stargazing!
  • You can pick fresh fruit from historic orchards in Fruita.
  • The park's name comes from its dome-shaped rock formations and "reef"-like ridges.

Capitol Reef National Park is an unforgettable experience combining stunning landscapes with rich cultural history. Explore everything from unique rock formations to ancient petroglyphs and historic orchards.
